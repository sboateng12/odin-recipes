Pumpkin Pancakes

Description

A pumpkin pancake is a type of pancake that is made with pumpkin puree, flour, eggs, milk, butter, and spices such as cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger. It is cooked on a griddle or a skillet until golden and fluffy, and served with maple syrup, whipped cream, or other toppings of your choice. A pumpkin pancake is a delicious and festive breakfast that is perfect for the fall season.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Spices (such as cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger)
  • Milk
  • Pumpkin puree
  • Eggs
  • Butter or oil
  • Maple syrup or other toppings

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and spices) in a large bowl.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients (pumpkin puree, milk, eggs, oil, and vanilla) in another bowl.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients and stir until well blended.
  4. Heat a lightly greased griddle or skillet over medium-high heat and drop about 1/4 cup of batter per pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook until golden on both sides.
  6. Serve with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, whipped cream, or nuts.
